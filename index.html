KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP SINCE 1992.

KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

Kakuma Refugee camp was established in the year 1992 with the displaced communities from south Sudan due to heavy war in the country, the main reception was Loki, meanwhile the kenyan govenment decided to settle the displaced communities Kakuma as the main Refugee center. Today kakuma is not only made of south Sudanese but many nationalities from the neigboring countries those that face war, hunger and persecution from the country of orgin. here is the link for kakuma.

INTER-CULTURAL ACTIIVITIES PRACTICE BY REFUGEES IN KAKUMA CAMP.

Kakuma is founded by UNHCR to help refugees in the following of the programs such as shelter, Education, and the basic needs such as water, food and accomondation. Please visit the UNHCR link to learn more about kakuma.

SEVERICES GIVEN TO REFUGEES IN KAKUAM CAMP.

Today kakuma has turned to be the most place where the following activities such as sport, cultural dance, commerical centers, vocational tranings take place and more other actiivities that take place in the camp.
